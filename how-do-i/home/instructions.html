How to Upgrade your current Cybertown 3D home

To upgrade your current 3D home, but stay in the same location, please follow these instructions:

  1. Go to your current home and move all the objects you own into your backpack for safe transport to your new location (Cybertown is not responsible for lost or broken items left in your former home).
  2. Find the button which is located below the control panel on the right, click it and follow the instructions to update and upgrade your home.

To upgrade your current 3D home and move to a new location, please follow these instructions:

  1. Go to your current home and move all the objects you own into your backpack for safe transport to your new location (Cybertown is not responsible for lost or broken items left in your former home).
  2. Click the button on the How Do I? page and follow the instructions to update and upgrade your home.
