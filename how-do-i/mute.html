Mute Voices

Mute All Voices | Mute An Individual

There will be times when you will want to turn off all the voices or maybe only one or two voices where you are in Cybertown. We have given you an easy way to do that so you can stay sane and enjoy your time in town.

Mute All Voices:

  1. Right click on your nickname under the People tab
  2. You will get a popup window
  3. Select Voice and then select Mute All
  4. Selecting the Mute feature will mute your own voice

To go back to hearing voices (he, he):

  1. Go back to Voice in the popup window
  2. Highlight the checked Mute All feature to deselect it and you'll hear voice chat again
Back to top

Mute An Individual:

  1. Right click on the person's nickname under the People tab
  2. You will get a popup window
  3. Select Voice and then select Mute. You will still see their text in the chat window, but you will not hear their voice anymore

You can also choose Ignore from the popup window which will mute their voice and hide their text in the chat window. To make these features active again, just go back to the popup window and highlight the checked feature to deselect it.

Happy chatting!
