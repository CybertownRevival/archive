So you want more City Cash and you're prepared to spend the time and do what it takes to get it? Cybertown Loves citizens like you! You are the kind of person who has made Cybertown such a great success online.

Employment Office:
You will find current employment opportunities at the Employment Office. To get to the Employment Office, click the Map link on the right and then the Employment Office link on the map.

Get a Job:
Once you get to the Employment Office, scroll down the right hand frame until you see the Job Offers image. Click the Job Offers image to see what jobs are currently available. If you find a position you like then apply directly to the advertisers home. Please do not reply on the message board. No one will know that you have applied for the job because they won't be looking for a reply there.

Here is one way to apply for a job:

Find the name of the person who has posted the job in the From: field.

Next, search for that name in the Citizen Directory. You can click on the linked nickname in the directory and it will take you to that home. When you get to their home you can leave a message in their Inbox about the job.

Happy job hunting!
