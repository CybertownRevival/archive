Visit a Home

Follow | Use Nickname | Search Directory

Visiting someone's home is one of the many exciting things you can do in Cybertown. There are a number of ways you can do this.

If you have met someone in Cybertown and they ask you to meet them in their house right away you can follow them like this:

  • Get the name of their house and then let them go there
  • Look at the list of places for the house name they gave you.
  • Select that place and right click to get a popup window
  • Choose Open
  • Voila! You are in that home

Now let's say you know the person's nickname but don't know the name of their house. You can try typing the following location infomation:

http://www.cybertown.com/home/[nickname of friend]

Back to top

Another situation you may run into is coming to Cybertown and only having partial information on a friend or aquaintance. In that case you would want to use the Cybertown Directory.

  • Find the Directory button on the Control Panel to the right and click it
  • Click Citizen to get to the Citizen Directory
  • Use one of the filters to search for the name or nickname of the person you are looking for
  • Clicking on a linked name will take you to their house
  • Then have FUN in Cybertown!
