#Placing Objects#

These simple instructions will get you started in decorating your home with the items you have in your backpack.

Remember: You can only have a maximum of 32 objects in your home.

First: Go to your 3D home by clicking the My Home button on the control panel and choosing Visit my House.
Next: Click the button. You can only place items in your home when you're in 3D.

Follow these steps once you're inside your home:

  1. Find your list of treasures in My Backpack.
  2. Right click on a list item and you'll get a pop up window:
    Cancel Menu: Will close the small pop-up window
    Drop: Will drop the object into the room you are currently in. Don't worry it won't break!
    Destroy: Vaporizes object. No messy cleanup.
    Properties: Will launch a small window with information on the object.
  3. Make sure you are in the room where you want to place your object before you drop it in your home.
  4. Once you have dropped an object it will disappear from your backpack list and appear in your objects list.
  5. Right click on a list item and you'll get a pop up window:
    Cancel Menu: Will close the small pop-up window
    Beam To: Guess what this one does.
    Move: Will launch the Move Control Panel so you can place your treasures in all the right places.
    Take: Will put an object into your backpack no matter where it is. This is especially useful if you moved an object and can't find it.
    Properties    : Will launch a small window with information on the object.
