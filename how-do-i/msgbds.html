Use the Message Boards

As you move through Cybertown, you may notice that each location has a messages button associated with it.

If you click on that button you will launch a popup window with interesting information about that area that other citizens want to share or announce. This is a great resource for finding out what people are doing in that area and finding out who the active visitors or citizens are.

If you would like to add your comments or reply to something on the message board then click the post button and you will be given a form for your comment.

Please be courteous and keep your comment appropriate to the location you are in.

We love hearing from you!
