<p></p><font color=white size=3>*Centering*</font><br></center>
<font color=yellow>< center ></font>
<font color=coral>This command will cause whatever follows to be centered in the next line in the page. You
 must always close with the</font>
<font color=yellow>< /center ></font>
<font color=coral>tag.For example:</font>
<font color=yellow>< center >This text is centered</font>
<font color=yellow> < /center ></font>
<font color=coral>will appear as:</font>
<P></P><center><font color=skyblue>This text is centered</font>
<P><font color=coral>Images can also be centered as well. Basically, anything can be centered.</font></p>
