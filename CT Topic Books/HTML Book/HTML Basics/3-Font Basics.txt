<b>Lesson #4 - Bold, Italic & Underlined</b><br>
<hr>
<p>
To make the font bold:<P>
<XMP><B>This will be in bold</B></XMP>
<B>This will be in bold</B><p>
To make the font italic:<p>
<XMP><I>This will be italic</I></XMP>
<I>This will be italic</I><p>
To underline your font:<p>
<XMP><U>This will be underlined</U></XMP>
<U>This will be underlined</U><p>
NOTE: Always close every code you open.<p>
<hr>
<b>Lesson #5 - Different Fonts</b><br>
<hr>
<p>
To make your font different, you must type <br>
the exact name of the font.<p>
<XMP><font face="Comic Sans MS">Type Text Here.</font></XMP>
<font face="Comic Sans MS">Type Text Here.</font><p>
NOTE: If someone doesn't have the font you <br>
choose on their computer they will not see that <br>
pretty font change. So don't get too exotic. <br>
Stick with some pretty basic fonts. Always<br> 
close your /font codes when you finish typing.<p>
I am so forgetful I sometimes type the open and <br>
the /close then start my typing in between.<p>
