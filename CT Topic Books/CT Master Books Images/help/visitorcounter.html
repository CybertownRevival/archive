6. Settings for "My Visitor Counter"

How to use "My Visitor Counter"

The Visitor Counter shows the number of visitors to your 3D house. Every time a friend or visitor comes to your house, the number increases by 1.
If somebody visits your house more than once in an hour, that counts as only one visit; if the visitor returns after an hour, then the counter increases again.

Use the "Edit information" button to change the info text that's displayed on the Visitor Counter page.
