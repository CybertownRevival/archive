4. Visiting friends with "My Beam Station"

4.1. Using "My Beam Station"

You can set up your personal beam station to link to other home pages.
The last 5 people who have visited your beam station are listed here. It also shows how often it has been used and when it was last changed.

Change a link with the "Edit link" button.

Insert a new link with the "Add link" button.
Use the "Delete link" button to remove a link.
