2.3. How to use "My Guest Book"

Give your personal guest book a title and a brief description. The last five people who've looked at the book are listed on the title page. The title page also shows how often the guest book has been read and when it was last changed.

Your guests can send their contributions to the server with the "Update" button.

Use the "Next page" button to go to the next page.

Use the "Previous page" button to go back a page.
You and your guests can edit the current page with the "Edit page" button. The guest book allows entries in HTML.
