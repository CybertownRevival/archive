5. Putting up pictures in the house
Select a picture frame: portrait, square or landscape.
Select the picture that you want to upload. Note that you can only upload pictures that have been saved in GIF or JPG format and are less than 30 KByte. Ideal form factors for the pictures are:
* Portrait 3 : 4
* Landscape 4 : 3
Upload the selected picture.
Note: If you still see the old picture in the browser window or 3D world after you uploaded the new picture, it might still be in your browser's cache.
To find out how to empty your cache (="Temporary Internet Files"), consult your browser's help file.
You may also have to empty the blaxxun Contact cache. After clicking once in the 3D world, press Ctrl+F9, select "Cache Settings" and empty the cache there by clicking on "Empty Cache now."
