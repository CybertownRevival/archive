1. Changing "My House" with the Homebuilder
In the entrance to your house you'll find the CONFIGURATOR, with which you can customize your house.
The CONFIGURATOR is visible only to you, NOT to your visitors.
All it takes is a click on the CONFIGURATOR to start your personal Homebuilder.



With the Homebuilder, you have a powerful tool that you can use to make the floors and ceilings, walls, windows and doors look the way you want them. Here's how to use the Homebuilder:

1.) Configuration with available textures

Select the room you want to configure in the selection list.
Activate the radio button of the object you want to configure (floors & ceilings, walls, or windows & door frames) by clicking on it with the left mouse button.
After activating the radio button, select the color OR texture you want to assign to your objects. After you click on a color OR texture, it will be allocated to the objects in the 3D world.
Repeat the selection "radio button" >> "color OR texture" for all 3 object groups..
If you're satisfied with all of your choices, select "Update" to save them.


2.) Configuration with your own textures

Start the Upload menu with a mouse click



* Use the "Browse" button to select the picture you want to load.
* Click on the "Preview" button to view the actual room with the selected picture. * Repeat "Browse" and "Preview" for all 3 configuration possibilites.
* Save all pictures with "Upload my textures."
* Click on the "Back" button to go back to the Homebuilder start page..
Important: use of your own textures presupposes that they are coded in *.gif or *.jpg format and have a size of 10 KByte or smaller.
