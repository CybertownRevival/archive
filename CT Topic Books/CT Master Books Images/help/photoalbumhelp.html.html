2.2. How to use "My Photo Album"

Give your personal photo album a title and a brief description of what it's about. The last five people who've looked at the album are listed on the title page. The title page also shows how often the photo album has been viewed and when it was last changed.

Click on the 3D object to edit your photo album. After you've finished editing your information, save the changes by clicking on "Update".

Use the "Next page" button to go to the next page.

Use the "Previous page" button to go back a page.
You can add a page with the "New page" button; you can add as many pages as you like and upload up to 4 pictures per page.
To edit the current page, use the "Edit page" button.
Note: If you still see the old picture in the browser window or 3D world after you uploaded the new picture, it might still be in your browser's cache.
To find out how to empty your cache (="Temporary Internet Files"), consult your browser's help file.
You may also have to empty the blaxxun Contact cache. After clicking once in the 3D world, press Ctrl+F9, select "Cache Settings" and empty the cache there by clicking on "Empty Cache now."
