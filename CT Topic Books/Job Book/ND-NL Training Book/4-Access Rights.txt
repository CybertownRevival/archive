<p align="center"><font color="#33FF00" size="4" face="Arial"><strong>ACCESS RIGHTS</strong></font></p>
<p> </p><p><font size="3" face="Arial">Jobs / Titles are enabled by the </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" size="3" face="Arial">granting of access rights</font>
<font size="3" face="Arial">. The access rights button become </font>
<font size="3" face="Arial">visible when you are granted rights to the tool yourself. It is </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" size="3" face="Arial">EXTREMELY IMPORTANT</font>
<font size="3" face="Arial"> when adding others to access rights that you make sure the</font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" size="3" face="Arial">EXACT  </font>
<font size="3" face="Arial">spelling of nicknames are entered. </font></p>
<center><img src="http://dannydking.org/games/guides/blockaccess.jpg" width="147" height="223"></center>
<p><font size="3" face="Arial">As per </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" size="3" face="Arial"><a href="http://www.gamescolony.org/" target=_new>Games Colony Guidelines</a></font>
<font size="3" face="Arial">, the following Access Rights Details should be adhered to:</font></p>
<h3><a name="BAccess"><font color="#8FF2FA" size="3" face="Arial">Block Level Access Rights:</font></a></h3>
<p><font face="Arial">1.- Block Leaders should list first their actual deputies (BD's) in the space provided
 beginning <u>TOP LEFT</u>. The Neighborhood Leader's name should </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">NOT </font>
<font face="Arial">appear on the list. </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">NO OTHER NAMES SHOULD BE LISTED</font>
<font face="Arial"> (including CD's or CL).</font></p>
<center><img src="http://dannydking.org/games/guides/blockdeputies.jpg" width="711" height="130"></center>
<p><font face="Arial">2.- Neighborhood Deputies' (NDs) names </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">MAY</font>
<font face="Arial"> be listed in the Block Leader Section if the block has no block leader. If the ND needs
 to access the block, and the block has a Block Leader, he or she will either put themself in a blank spot
 or remove one of the BD nicknames, perform the duties and replace the nickname when complete. Under NO
 circumstances should any staff member be in access unless they work there</font></p>
<p><font face="Arial">3.- In the access panel you should check the Com Tech and City Council job positions
 only. </font>
 <font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial"><u>NO OTHER POSITION SHOULD BE CHECKED.</u></font></p>
<center><img src="http://dannydking.org/games/guides/accesspanel.jpg" width="794" height="402"></center>
<p><font face="Arial">4.- Note that there are several different pages of Access Rights. Normally, </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6"> you should never need to change the information</font> on pages other than the Owner
Access Rights.  However, often we find that these other pages have been changed and we need to correct the
 entries.</p>
<p>There are a total of <font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">7 different Access Rights Pages</font>
<font face="Arial">. The 4 main pages are the Owner, Read, Action and Change Pages.  The Owner Rights page
 is the one that is displayed when you first open the Access Rights. This is the screen where you list the
 Block Deputies, and checkmark the 3 jobs listed above.</font></p>
<p>As mentioned above, there are a total of 7 Access Rights Screens. There are the 4 primary ones, and on
 the Read, Action and Change Screens, there are 
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">Chat Access Rights</font>
<font face="Arial"> which can be set also.  As you can see in the image below, under the Update Access
 title, there is a Chat Access button. Clicking this button will bring up the secondary Chat Access rights
 pages, on the Read, Action and Change Screens.</font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">There should be no names listed or titles checked on any access rights 
pages other than the Owner's Rights page.</font></p>
<p><center><img src="http://dannydking.org/games/guides/accessscreen.jpg" width="815" height="470"></center></p>

<h3><a name="NAccess"><font color="#8FF2FA" size="3" face="Arial">Neighborhood Level Access Rights:</font></a></h3>
<p><font face="Arial">1.- Neighborhood Leaders need only list their deputies.  It is not necessary to list
 the CL or CDs on this page.</font></p>
<p><font face="Arial">2.- As in the Block level access, the positions of Com Tech and City Council job
 positions </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">ONLY </font>
<font face="Arial">should be checked.</font></p>
<center><img src="http://dannydking.org/games/guides/update.jpg" width="805" height="229"></center>
<p><font face="Arial">3.- ALWAYS click on the UPDATE button, </font><font color="#EAE7A6"
face="Arial">NEVER </font><font face="Arial">hit the ENTER key.</font></p>
