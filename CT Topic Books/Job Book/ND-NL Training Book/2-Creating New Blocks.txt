<p align="center"><font color="#33FF00" size="4" face="Arial"><strong>CREATING NEW BLOCKS</strong></font></p>
<p></p><p align="left"><font size="3" face="Arial">As per Games Colony Guidelines, </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" size="3" face="Arial">ONLY</font><font size="3" face="Arial"> the </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" size="3" face="Arial">NL and up</font><font face="Arial"> can </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">CREATE A NEW BLOCK</font>
<font face="Arial">. However, before doing so, approval must be sought by </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial"><strong><u>PROPOSING THE NAME OF THE NEW BLOCK TO THE CL</u></strong>.</font>
<font face="Arial"> Once approval has been given, the NL may open the block, assign a block leader,
 announce it's opening etc, as she or he sees fit.</font></p>
<p><font face="Arial">Block names should keep within the theme of the neighborhood and suggestions can be
 received from the CL, competitions, word of mouth, etc as the NL sees fit, subject of course, to CL
 approval.</font></p>
<p><font face="Arial">In order to add a new block, whilst at the neighborhood level, the NL clicks in the
 grid where the new block is to appear.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font face="Arial"><img src="http://dannydking.org/games/guides/hoodmap1.jpg" width="535" height="300"></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial">Upon doing so a </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">Block Wizard</font>
<font face="Arial"> replace the view that </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">allows the locking or unlocking </font>
<font face="Arial">of blocks. It is in this same space that the NL might </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">rename the block</font>
<font face="Arial"> or </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">change the 2D icon</font>
<font face="Arial"> that represents it (changing the icon does not necessarily change the map of the block).</font></p>
<p align="center"><font face="Arial"><img src="http://dannydking.org/games/guides/hoodmap2.jpg" width="502" height="511"></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial">Until the flag for Block Usage is changed to </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">free for access</font>
<font face="Arial">, the block will not be shown on the Neighborhood Map.  The block name </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" face="Arial">approved by the CL</font>
<font face="Arial"> is entered in the Name Field.  The block will always require a name listed as Block
 Owner. Initially it may say the NL's name, however this will be replaced by the incoming BL name when
 appropriate.</font></p>
<p><font face="Arial">The Colony Leader has final approval of all block names, additions or removals.
 Under no circumstances should unauthorized changing of block names, additions or removals occur.</font></p>
