<p align="center"><font color="#33FF00" size="4" face="Arial"><strong>SAMPLE BL / BD GUIDELINES</strong></font></p>
<p align="left"> </p>
<p align="left"><font size="3" face="Arial">Below is an </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" size="3" face="Arial">example</font>
<font size="3" face="Arial"> of some sample guidelines that were developed to meet a particular
 neighborhoods requirements of how they wanted things done. Their training site hadn't been developed,
 so the guidelines served as a temporary information base. </font>
<font color="#EAE7A6" size="3" face="Arial">It is offered here as a resource to pick & choose from and can
 be used, adapted, modified or totally forgotten about :-)</font></p>
<p align="center"> </p>
<p align="center"><font color="#8FF2FA" size="4" face="Arial"><b>BLOCK LEADER & BLOCK DEPUTY <br>
GUIDELINES</b></font></p>
<p align="center"><font size="3" face="Arial"><b>These Guidelines are to be used in conjunction with <br>
Cybertown and Colony Guidelines.</b></font></p>
<p><hr></p>
<p><font color="#8FF2FA" size="3" face="Arial"><b>ADMINISTRATIVE DUTIES</b></font></p>
<ul>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">Block Leaders (BL) are responsible for making sure ALL NEW RESIDENTS TO THE
BLOCK are welcomed by way of "Message to All", their inbox , and if you would like the block Message board.</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">BL's are responsible for submitting a Block Report every two weeks to their
assigned ND (dates confirmed with them). The report format should follow the guideline given.</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">BL's are responsible for maintaining and updating the Block Responsibility
 List and submitting it to their assigned ND upon changes of task allocation.</font></li>
</ul>
<blockquote>
    <p><font size="3" face="Arial">It is recommended that at least one other BD knows these procedures in
	 case the BL is not available to do these two reports.</font></p>
</blockquote>
<p><hr></b></p>
<p><font color="#8FF2FA" size="3" face="Arial"><b>EMPLOYMENT & TRAINING</b></font></p>
<ul>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">BL's are responsible for hiring, firing & training their BD’s. Assigned
ND's should be consulted on this whenever possible.</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">All staff must read through Colony Guidelines and the training section from
 the Games Colony Training Guides.</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">It is the BL's responsibility to ensure tasks are allocated to and completed
 by BD's (see Block Responsibility List).</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">It is asked that BD's give at least 48 hrs notice for resignation or
 promotion to their BL.</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">It is asked that BL's give at least 7 days notice for resignation o
r promotion to their assigned ND.</font></li>
</ul>
<p><hr></b></p>
<p><font color="#8FF2FA" size="3" face="Arial"><b>COMMUNICATION</b></font></p>
<ul>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">BL's & BD's should communicate frequently.</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">BL's must submit their email contact details (suitable for distribution to
 NL / ND's and CT use) to the Records ND (see ND Admin List if unsure).</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">BL's should have email contact details of their BD's and vice versa.</font></li>
</ul>
<p><hr></b></p>
<p><font color="#8FF2FA" size="3" face="Arial"><b>ABSENCE</b></font></p>
<ul>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">If for any reason a BL is going to be absent for up to 3 days they should
 inform a BD. Any longer than 3 days then please report it to your assigned ND (so we know that you have to
 be gone and can help care for your block).</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">Only the DCL is to delete homes and free home sites (unless alternative
 arrangement has been approved by the CL).</font></li>
</ul>
<p><hr></b></p>
<p><font color="#8FF2FA" size="3" face="Arial"><b>STAFF HOMES</b></font></p>
<ul>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">All staff need to have (BL) or (BD) on house name.</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">Wherever possible BL should be first home on the block list followed by
 BD's.</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">All staff need to have an image on their home.</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">All staff should have a decorated message board and inbox.</font></li>
</ul>
<p><hr></b></p>
<p><font color="#8FF2FA" size="3" face="Arial"><b>HTML</b></font></p>
<ul>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">Staff should encourage the use of accepting HTML by residents on message
 boards but must remember when sending messages to all that many don't have this set, as such, especially
 new residents who are not familiar with it.</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">Each block should have a decorated block MB (in keeping with the theme of
 the block name). Assistance is available.</font></li>
<li><font size="3" face="Arial">It is preferred if blocks have a suitable web page - perhaps help topics,
 details of staff, links, etc. Although the MB may address such ideas instead.</font></li>
</ul>
<p><hr></b></p>
<p><font color="#8FF2FA" size="3" face="Arial"><b>FUN</b></font></p>
<p><font size="3" face="Arial">FUN now this is a very important part of this job…If you can’t or won’t have
 fun you will be punished. Remember I DO have a cattle prod.. :).</font></p>
<p><font size="3" face="Arial">But seriously, this is ALL supposed to be fun. Everyone has a real life,
 everyone has enough pressures and problems in them, and to many this is their escape. If you're not having fun or there is a problem to be overcome (be
it requiring leave of absence for Real Life reasons or whatever) then please let us know. </font></p>
<p><font color="#EAE7A6" size="3" face="Arial">We are all part of a team and family and are here for each other!!</font></p>
