<p><a name="w18"><strong><font size="3">"Colony Menu"</font></strong></a></p>
<font size="3">
<P>The <B><font color=yellow>Infomation</font></B> button is a very useful tool, it shows<BR>
who is in CL and CD access.<BR>
<P>The <B><font color=yellow>Portal</font></B> button shows a list of related online games<BR>
links.<BR>
<P><B><font color=yellow>Find Home</font></B> tells you how many homes in each hood and how<BR>
many are free.<BR>
<P><B><font color=yellow>Statistics</font></B> tell you lots of details about the hood and the<BR>
colony.<BR>
<P><B><font color=yellow>Inbox</font></B> is for people to contact the Games "C" level staff and<BR>
the staff can also use it to communicate.<BR>
<P>The <B><font color=yellow>Messages</font></B> button opens the message board where you can post<BR>
and view messages.<BR>
<P>The <B><font color=yellow>Vote</font></B> button brings up the colony voting board, you can<BR>
vote and start votes there.<BR>
<P>The <B><font color=yellow>Moderate</font></B> button is for when there is a big event going on<BR>
and you need to accept everything that is said.<BR>
<P>The <B><font color=yellow>Inbox to All</font></B> button sends an inbox to every inbox in the colony.<BR>
<P>The <B><font color=yellow>Message to All</font></B> sends messages to the hoods message boards.<BR>
<P><B><font color=yellow>Update</font></B> changes the information page. Only the CL and CDL should<BR>
use this.<BR>
<P>The <B><font color=yellow>Fund Box</font></B> button is where the colony funds are stored. You can<BR>
put money in but only the CL and DCL can take money out.<BR>
