AAPD - 2000 (Aromatic Anti-personnel Device)

Another very annoying weapon, the AAPD-2000 is a stink bomb launcher. It explodes upon impact leaving a green gas cloud in a 10 meter radius. The smell is so unbearable that anyone standing within the perimeter at the moment of impact will be forced to beam out. The odor is even worse than a fart of the dreaded Swok of the Sinead Galaxy so be warned!
