General Rules!

Cybertown is a family-oriented town that includes children among its visitors, so a Club must be respectful by not allowing pornography, sexually-explicit links or encouraging crime or attacks against other people. Bigotry, racism, hatred, profanity, defamatory or harrassing behavior of any kind will not be accepted in the Clubs. Clubs may also not violate copyrights or provide instructional information about illegal activities.
When chatting in the Clubs, all Constitutional rules apply.
