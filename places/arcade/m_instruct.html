1. Click the start button to begin anew game
  2. Once the game begins, moles will pop up out of their holes
  3. Click a mole hole to aim the Bopper.  The bopper will automatically bop the hole you click.
  4. If you bop a mole before it goes back into it's hole, you score a point.
  5. You have 20 seconds to bop all the moles you can
