Spanish 21 is just like regular blackjack with a few exceptions...

Here are the different rules:

  • THERE ARE NO 10's IN THE SHOE! This game is played with 6 decks containing 2-9, J, Q, K, A.
  • Player 21 ALWAYS wins. Player Blackjack ALWAYS wins 3/2.
  • You may double down once at any time.
  • You may take as many hits as you like (until you bust) after splitting a pair.
  • You may surrender after seeing your first two cards losing only half your bet. Surrender against Dealer Blackjack NOT allowed.
  • You may rescue after doubling down (Take back doubled portion, lose your original bet).
  • Added bonuses for different card combinations:
    • 5 card 21 pays 3-2
    • 6 card 21 pays 2-1
    • 7 card 21 pays 3-1
    • 6-7-8 mixed pays 3-2
    • 6-7-8 suited pays 2-1
    • 6-7-8 spaded pays 3-1
    • 7-7-7 mixed pays 3-2
    • 7-7-7 suited pays 2-1
    • 7-7-7 spaded pays 3-1
    • 7-7-7 suited with dealer up card any 7 pays $5000 for a bet of $25 or more. Pay $1000 if $5 >= bet < $25.
  • You may only split 3 times (total of four hands).
  • Bonuses do not apply to doubled hands.
  • Dealer hits soft 17.
