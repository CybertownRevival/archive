1. Click on green portion of bowling alley, and hold down mouse button. Move the mouse left or right to place the ball in its startingposition on the lane.
  2. Click on ball, and hold down mouse button. Move the mouse left or right SLIGHTLY to aim the ball inthe direction you would like it to travel.
  3. CONTINUE TO HOLD THE MOUSE BUTTON DOWN. A pointerwill begin to sweep across the top of the ball. It will continue to sweep as long as the mouse button is held down. This will determine how much hook is placed on the ball based on its distance from your aim.
  4. Release the mouse button to bowl.
