A community is only as good as its people, we need residents who are active in the community for various functions.

By choosing a specific function, you will also be able to get more money, buy better houses as they are released and much more so you should be saving some right now!

For a more complete briefing come to the first meeting held in City Hall, see the announcement page for more information. Here are the various functions we are looking volunteers for:

COMMUNITY LEADERS - handle the day-to-day running of the community.

Functions are:

i) Helping neophytes and residents - this includes such functions as Community Guides who help by answering questions, helping them find things in the community, giving tours, helping them build homes,  manning the Bulletin Boards, hosting chat sessions and helping with any problems.

ii) Acting as an information liaison between the community "Founding Fathers" and the community - this includes such things as providing information and announcements to the community about events, guidelines, news, running the community newspaper, creating a newsletter, letting the community know about ColonyCity appearances in the media and letting the "Founding Fathers" get needed information from the community.

iii) Helping residents get to know each other and bringing together residents with similar interests - this includes such things as organizing and running Special Interest Bulletin Boards and Chat Discussion Groups, creating community events and get-togethers, games, competitions, awards for best houses and other created objects.

iv) Helping expand and update the community - this includes such things as building new areas, buildings, attractions, objects - including furniture and avatars - and features to add to the community, helping promote the community, repairing and maintaining the community etc.

v) Maintaining order in the community - this includes such things as checking into the chat rooms areas and ensuring that people follow the guidelines regarding no profanity and disruptive behavior and no offensive images in buildings etc. This includes Community Leaders being Cybercops, as necessary.

WORLD GUIDES

Functions are very simple, you are basically in-charge of a specific world, you take care of the place, dust it, keep it clean, greet people as they come in, answer questions relating to the world..(Like, what is this City Hall world?)

Your job is also to entertain people, keep the conversation alive and interesting.

Presently we need world guides for the following worlds:

                              - CITY PLAZA

                              - CITY HALL

                              - THE CAFE

                              - CITY AIRPORT

                             - CULTURAL CENTER

Notes:

All those functions requires people who are responsible, are very trustworthy and will indeed do the functions they have choosed. In the near future, the City Council will review the appointments and make them more permanents. The idea is to have the community being able to run itself, hence we need a lot of responsible people..it is not only a challenge but it is pioneering field.

As of June 1998 we are looking at 10 Community Leaders and 5 world guides. Applicants should send an e-mail and explain why they are interested in the position, what experience they have with online community and similar function in the past, what is their computer experience (programing, VRML expertise, graphics, writing, etc...) and what they would like to achieve.

It must be understood that the primary duty of any Colony City function will be to help residents and visitors.
