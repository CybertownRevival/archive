Y U R Here

 

You were placed in the Cybertown Jail as a result of your breaking one or more of the Cybertown Constitutional rules. When you immigrated into our beloved City you agreed to abide to all the Constitutional Rules that were set forth.

The offense committed did not warrant a complete exclusion from the city. However, it will behoove you to now take the time to read all the polices that we have supplied here so you have a better understanding of what is appropriate behavior when you return to the City with full citizen privileges.

While you are in the Cybertown Jail:

  1. You will not receive the daily experience points and citycash deposits that law abiding citizens receive.
  2. You will not be allowed access to any other part of the City.
  3. You will not have access to many of the personal features that would otherwise be available to you.
  4. You may be subject to motivational speakers to help in your rehabilitation.
  5. If you use bad language while in the Jail, your jail term could be prolonged.

When your jail time has been completed, you will once again be allowed full access to the City. Any transgressions that may arise from you after you have done your "time" may result in a total exile from Cybertown.
