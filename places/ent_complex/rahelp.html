How to enjoy Real Player Content in the ePlex

Basic Software Requirements | Q & A | Extra Strength Support

The information on this page is being provided to help you enjoy the RealAudio and RealVideo content in the Cybertown ePlex.

Cybertown is using the streaming technology of the free Real G2 Player which will only work with the current version of blaxxun Contact (4.2). Please make sure you and your friends have the most recent software/upgrades of the plug-ins for optimum performance:

  • The free blaxxun Contact plug-in
  • The RealPlayer G2, free, plays all Real content
An FYI:

In locations containing RealPlayer G2 content,  you may notice that you no longer have the text-to-voice chat working. That feature was turned off in these locations so that it wouldn't interfere with the RealPlayer G2.
